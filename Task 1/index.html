Q. Can we accept calls on it? Is it good for calls, meetings and work from home?

Yes, a single press on the multi-function button allows the user to easily accept and reject calls. they are designed for great clarity and comfort during meetings and important calls.

Q. Can we adjust the length of the wire?

No, the length of the wire cannot be adjusted.

Q. Can we use it while putting the headphones on charge?

Yes, one can use the device while charging it at the same time.

You can change tracks, adjust volume, take and reject calls, redial, and access voice assistant with the inline buttons.
